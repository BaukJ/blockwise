# For students

When a teacher adds you to a timetable, it appears under **Your timetables** in the
student view (sign up or log in with the email your teacher used). You may also receive
a one-time **magic link** that lets you fill in your choices without an account.

## Filling in your choices

- Rank your subject choices in order of preference, plus any backups.
- Some timetables have rules (e.g. a particular choice must come from a set of
  subjects) — the form only offers valid options and tells you if something's wrong.
- Once you submit you can't change your choices, so check carefully. Watch the
  **deadline** if one is shown.

## After processing

When your teacher finalises the timetable you'll see your allocation per block. If they
open reassignment, you can swap into another subject in the same block that still has
free space — your original allocation is always kept on record.
